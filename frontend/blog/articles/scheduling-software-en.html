Most widely used program for large, complex projects globally, especially in oil & gas and infrastructure sectors.

  • Handles thousands of activities
  • Advanced Resource Management
  • Multi-project and Portfolio management
  • Detailed customizable reports
  • ERP integration

Overview:

Most popular choice for medium projects, user-friendly and integrated with Microsoft products.

Advantages:

  • Familiar interface for Office users
  • Easy to learn and use
  • Excel and SharePoint integration
  • Reasonable cost
  • Suitable for medium projects

Disadvantages:

  • Limited for very large projects
  • Less advanced resource management
  • No central database as powerful as P6
