Characteristics:

  • Design is the responsibility of employer/consultant.

Conditions of Contract for Plant and Design-Build

Usage:

Projects where contractor handles both design and construction.

Characteristics:

  • Design is contractor's responsibility.
  • Employer provides performance requirements (Employer's Requirements).
  • Contractor submits technical proposal (Contractor's Proposal).
  • Payment method: Usually lump sum.
  • Greater risk transfer to contractor.

Official Name:

Conditions of Contract for EPC/Turnkey Projects

Usage:

Turnkey projects where contractor bears complete responsibility.

Characteristics:

  • Contractor responsible for everything: design, procurement, construction.
  • No "Engineer" - employer manages contract directly.
  • Very high risks on contractor.
  • Fixed price and determined delivery date.
  • Very limited price adjustments.
