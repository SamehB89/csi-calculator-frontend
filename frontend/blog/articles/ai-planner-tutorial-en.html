How to Use the AI Planner to Manage Your Project?

January 2, 2026 5 min read

🤖 Introduction

Imagine having an expert engineering assistant available 24/7, who understands your requests in natural language and proposes crews, equipment, and duration instantly. This is exactly what the free AI Planner on our website does.

Step 1: Access the Tool

Click on the "AI Planner" icon in the navigation menu or click here. You will see a simple and easy-to-use chat interface.

Step 2: Write Your Request (Prompt)

You don't need to write code or fill out complex forms. Just describe what you want to do.

Examples of prompts:

  • "Pouring isolated footings, 50 cubic meters of plain concrete"
  • "Building a red brick wall, area of 200 square meters"
  • "Installing floor ceramic tiles, 100 meters"
💡 Pro Tip: The more specific you are about the quantity and type of work, the more accurate the results.

Step 3: Get an Instant Plan

Once you send the message, the AI will analyze your request, match it with the CSI database, and reply with detailed information including:

  • Required Activities: (Carpentry, steel fixing, pouring, etc.)
  • Proposed Crew: Number of workers and supervisors for each activity.
  • Required Equipment: Do you need a mixer? Vibrator? Crane?
  • Duration: How many days will this work take?

Step 4: Refine and Customize

The results are an initial proposal. You can always ask for adjustments, for example:

  • "Can this be done in 2 days instead of 4?"
  • "Calculate the cost if the daily wage is 150 EGP"

Why Use AI Planner?

  • Speed: A full plan in seconds.
  • Accuracy: Based on global standards.
  • Ease: A chat interface familiar to everyone.

📝 Conclusion

The AI Planner is your first step towards digitizing your engineering work. Try it now and save hours of manual planning.
