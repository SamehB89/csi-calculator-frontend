BIM Benefits for Owner/Client

🎯 Better Visualization

  • Realistic project view before construction
  • VR/AR walkthroughs for decisions
  • Fewer surprises during execution

💰 Superior Cost Control

  • Accurate quantity takeoff from model
  • Instant cost impact of changes
  • Design alternatives by cost

⚠️ Risk Reduction

  • Early clash detection
  • Fewer claims and disputes
  • More reliable schedule

🏢 Long-term Value

  • As-Built model for maintenance
  • Plan future renovations
  • Digital Twin of building

📋 Owner Case Study: Healthcare Facility

A 50,000 m² hospital project used BIM from design through construction:

  • $2.1M saved through early clash detection
  • 3 months reduced from schedule via better coordination
  • 150+ clashes resolved before construction
  • As-Built model used for 10+ years of facility management
